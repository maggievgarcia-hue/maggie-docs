# Troubleshooting Guide

This sample shows structured problem-solving documentation for end users. Information is organized for quick scanning and practical application.

---

## Issue 1: App Will Not Launch

### **Possible Causes**
- Outdated operating system  
- Damaged installation files  
- App requires update  

### **Solutions**
1. Restart your device  
2. Ensure your OS meets the minimum requirements  
3. Download the latest version from the Clarity Notes website  
4. Reinstall the application  

---

## Issue 2: Notes Are Not Syncing

### **Possible Causes**
- No internet connection  
- Syncing disabled  
- Account conflict  

### **Solutions**
1. Confirm you are connected to Wi-Fi  
2. Open **Settings → Sync** and ensure the toggle is ON  
3. Log out and back into your account  
4. If syncing is still failing, select **Force Sync**  

---

## Issue 3: Password Reset Email Not Received

### **Solutions**
1. Check your spam or junk folder  
2. Ensure your email address is correct  
3. Wait 10 minutes — email delivery can be delayed  
4. Select **Resend Email**  
5. If still unsuccessful, contact **support@claritynotes.example.com**
